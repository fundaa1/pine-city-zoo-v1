RHINO-BEETLE

The rhino beetle is a genus of beetles in the family Scarabaeidae. It is the only genus in the subfamily Dynastinae. The rhinoceros beetle is a large, robust, and heavy-bodied beetle, with a length of 20–100 mm (0.79–3.94 in). The rhinoceros beetle is a member of the family Scarabaeidae, which is the largest family of beetles.
