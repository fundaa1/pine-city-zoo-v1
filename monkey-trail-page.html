ANIMALS MAP PLACES

MONKEY TRAIL

Whether you choose to walk or ride, you will find the monkey trail very challenging. Take the 30 minute or 1 hour trail.

Be prepared to get wet and muddy!

The trail is suitable for:

  • Adults
  • Children over 12 years
  • Children under 12 years must be accompanied by an adult
  • Wheelchair users

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

Monkey Trail is at the eastern side of the zoo.
