Visitor Information

Everything you need to know to plan your visit to Pine City Zoo. We look forward to welcoming you!

Opening Hours

Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Saturday - Sunday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Public Holidays: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM (Please check our homepage for specific holiday hours)

Ticket Prices

Adults: $25

Children (3-12 years): $15

Seniors (65+): $20

Children under 3: Free

Group discounts available for parties of 10 or more. Please contact us for details.

Getting Here

Address: 123 Zoo Lane, Pine City, PC 12345

By Car: Ample parking available on-site. Parking fee: $5 per vehicle.

By Bus: Bus route 42 stops directly outside the zoo entrance.

Accessibility

Pine City Zoo is committed to providing an accessible environment for all our guests.

  • Wheelchair rentals available at the entrance.
  • Most pathways are wheelchair and stroller friendly.
  • Accessible restrooms are located throughout the zoo.
  • Service animals are welcome.

Zoo Rules & Guidelines

  • Please do not feed the animals. They have specialized diets.
  • For the safety of our animals and guests, pets are not allowed inside the zoo (service animals excepted).
  • Smoking is permitted only in designated areas.
  • Please stay on marked pathways.
  • Commercial photography requires prior permission.

Facilities

  • Restrooms (including accessible and baby changing facilities)
  • First Aid station (near the main entrance)
  • Gift Shop
  • Food and beverage outlets (see Places page)
  • Picnic areas

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring my own food and drinks?

Yes, you are welcome to bring your own food and drinks to enjoy in our designated picnic areas.

Are there lockers available?

Yes, lockers are available for rent near the main entrance.

What happens if it rains?

Pine City Zoo remains open rain or shine! Many of our animal habitats have indoor viewing areas. Check the Weather page for current conditions.
